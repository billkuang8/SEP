About us Who we're looking for
Although UC Berkeley is full of talented individuals, the entrepreneurship landscape has been traditionally underdeveloped with few organizations dedicated to actively cater to the needs of the entrepreneurial minded. Sigma Eta Pi is our answer to this discrepancy. Diversity is our key strength and we are open to students of all majors. More than anything else, we are looking for individuals with drive, passion, ideas and the skills to execute them. Engineers, business majors, graphic artists are all welcome in Sigma Eta Pi.
